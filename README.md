# GenericBlockGame

GenericBlockGame is my implementation of a generic block puzzle game that runs in the browser.

[Play now in browser](https://goodgui.github.io/GenericBlockGame/)

## How to Play

1. Drag a piece from the tray onto the board.
2. Pieces cannot overlap filled squares or extend beyond the board.
3. Fill a complete row, column, or outlined board section to clear it.
4. Use every tray piece to receive a new tray.
5. The game ends when none of the remaining tray pieces can be placed.

Clearing one section earns 9 points. Consecutive clears build a streak, and clearing multiple sections with one piece awards a larger multiplier.

Use **Undo** to reverse moves made during the current tray. Open **Settings** to adjust board and tray sizes, piece frequencies, custom pieces, drag offsets, or saved data.

Your current game, settings, and top score are saved in the browser memory.

Enable **Time Trial** in Settings to play against a configurable countdown. The timer starts when the first piece is placed
